Who We Are

Cinder Health Care is a trusted healthcare and support provider dedicated to helping individuals of all ages live safely and independently in their own homes.

Our Mission

To deliver consistent, high-quality care that empowers people to remain independent, safe, and connected in their own homes. We strive to ease the burden on families while improving the wellbeing of every client we serve.

What We Do

Discover personalized care solutions designed around you. Let us be your trusted partner in maintaining independence, comfort, and dignity at home.

Personal Care & Daily Support

Start each day with confidence. Our compassionate caregivers provide dignified assistance with personal hygiene, grooming, medication management, and nutritious meal preparation—all tailored to your preferences and schedule.

Home Support & Maintenance

Keep your home as comfortable as you are. We handle housekeeping, grocery shopping, errands, and regular wellness visits so you can focus on what matters most—living your life to the fullest.

Companionship & Respite Care

Never feel alone again. Enjoy meaningful conversations, community outings, and engaging activities while giving your family caregivers the well-deserved break they need to recharge.

Specialist & Complex Care

Receive expert care for complex conditions. Our specially trained professionals provide comprehensive support for autism, learning disabilities, rehabilitation, and specialized medical needs with the expertise your situation demands.

Additional Support Services

To make daily life even easier, we also provide a range of extra support options designed with your comfort and peace of mind in focus.

Overnight Care

Carers available throughout the night for reassurance, assistance, or monitoring. Sleep peacefully knowing professional support is always nearby when you need it most.

Live-In Care

A dedicated carer living in your home to provide round-the-clock support while maintaining your independence in familiar surroundings. Stay in the place you love most.

Palliative & End-of-Life Care

Compassionate support focused on comfort, dignity, and peace of mind for clients and their families during sensitive times. Care delivered with the utmost respect and understanding.
